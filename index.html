A little bit about me

Hello... my name is Riyaaz; I am 39 years old, I live in Cape Town, South Africa and have a 5year old daughter who epitomizes my whole world.

I am currently a sales consultant at an ornamental steel manufacturing and imports company.I am quiet (or should I say shy) by nature though completely the opposite when around people I am comfortable with, like my friends, immediate family members and colleagues.

I have always had a love for all things Tech & DIY so undertake to do almost everything I need done, by myself.Apart from work, I spend most of my days in-doors, even though I enjoy being out in nature or the ocean, I find myself mostly indoors, either gaming on my PC, watching a movie or browsing the internet doing research or just plain old reading.That is of course until recently, when I realized that I would actually like to learn to code & make a career of it in the tech industry. I am very excited about working in the tech industry as it has actually always been a very big dream of mine, ever since I first discovered computers during my early high school years, when windows 3.1. was the operating system in use.Needless to say: most of my time, is now spent studying and learning to code, and I thoroughly enjoy it.
